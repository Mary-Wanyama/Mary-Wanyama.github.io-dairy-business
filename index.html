our cows page

these are some of the breeds available in our farm. they all live in a burn where they are zero grazed. once in a while they live the burn to walk around the farm.

Ayrshire

They are known for vigorous and efficient milk production.
They can produce up to 50 litres of milk in a day.
The female one can weigh up to 600kgs and the male 900kgs.
The most exiting part about this breed is that they can maintain
health.

Guernsey

Guernsey cows exist in between the jersey and the holstein breeds
They are known for producing quality milk.
They produce up to 20liters of milk in a day.
The cross breeded ones give up to 10liters.
The milk contain, high vitamins, proteins, calcium, cream.
All of them is adequate amounts.
Heifers weigh up to 600kgs and bulls 700kgs

Holstein

Holstein freshian cows are known for producing alot of milk.
They can produce up to 60liters per day.
The most exiting fact about them is that they grow and mature faster
the size of a heifer is usually 580kgs.
